About Me

Dr.K.A.Vidhya is currently working as Teaching Fellow in the Department of Information Science and Technology, College of Engineering, Guindy Campus, Anna University, Chennai. She has around 3 years of teaching experience, 4.5 of industry experience and 4.5 years of Research experience. She was the course instructor of various subjects like Complier Engineering, Database Management Systems and Advanced Operating Systems, however she is also interested in coding and creating some new research for prevailing societal problems. She has organized and participated in various events which bagged her the award of Best ALL Rounder in her undergraduate course. She has participated in various seminars and workshops conducted at the international and national level. She has published several research papers in international journals and presented research contributions in national and international conference. Dr.K.A.Vidhya did her B.E Computer Science and Engg from Annamalai University (Second Rank Holder) and M.Tech from Pondicherry University, she secured a gold medal followed by full-time Ph.D from Anna University. Her research interest includes Machine Learning, AI, Text and Image Processing with Deep Learning.
